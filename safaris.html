Things to Know Before Booking a Safari

  • Safari travel is immersive and nature-focused
  • Daily schedules revolve around wildlife activity
  • Light packing and neutral clothing are recommended
  • Travel insurance and proper documentation are essential
  • Each safari itinerary should be customized

Proper planning makes a significant difference in comfort, expectations, and overall experience.
