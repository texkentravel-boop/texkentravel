Why Choose a Safari

Kenya & The Masai Mara

Kenya is one of Africa’s most iconic safari destinations, with the Masai Mara known for the Great Migration.

What to Know Before You Go

  • Safaris are guided and thoughtfully paced
  • Seasons affect wildlife viewing
  • Comfort varies by lodge

What to Pack

  • Neutral clothing
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Sun protection
  • Camera or binoculars
